Jujutsu Kaisen

High school student Yuuta Okkotsu is plagued by violent misfortunes due to bullying, but he holds a powerful curse that allows him to seek brutal revenge. The source of the curse is Rika Orimoto, a shadow from his tragic past that looms as a lethal threat to anyone who does him wrong. Satoru Gojou, a sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu High, recognizes Yuuta's unique potential and tries to help him turn his curse into a force for good. However, Yuuta struggles to find his place among his talented classmates, including Toge Inumaki, Maki Zenin, and Panda. While Yuuta tries to use Rika's power to aid his friends, Suguru Getou, a curse user, seeks to exploit Rika's strength and eliminate all non- jujutsu users from the world. Yuuta must fight against Getou's genocidal plot with the help of his comrades.

Silent Voice

As a young student in elementary school, Shouya Ishida found joy in partaking in the cruelest forms of entertainment, earning himself a reputation as a mischievous troublemaker. However, when Shouko Nishimiya, a deaf student, entered their class, she was singled out as the object of their bullying, despite her mother's intervention. As a result, Shouko was eventually forced to leave the school, leaving Shouya to bear the consequences of his actions. Unaided by the teachers, he was abandoned by his peers throughout his elementary and middle school years. Now in his third year of high school, Shouya still bears the scars of his past transgressions. But driven by a desire for redemption, he sets out to make amends with Shouko while battling his inner demons. This inspiring story, Koe no Katachi, follows Shouya's path of redemption as he faces his past and strives to mend his strained relationship with Shouko.
